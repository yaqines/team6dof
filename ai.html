AI & Autonomy

From perception to decision-making in mission-critical scenarios.

1. Image Capture

Camera captures real-time mission images across the search area.

2. Preprocessing

Frames are cleaned and tagged with GPS and orientation data.

3. Detection

AI detects targets and filters out false positives.

4. Localization

Convert detections into real-world coordinates.

5. Decision

System decides mapping, revisit, or payload delivery.

Detection Philosophy

The system prioritizes reliability over risky decisions. Multiple confirmations are required before triggering mission actions, ensuring stable competition performance.

Mapping Output

AI supports risk mapping and target identification. Output is designed to be usable for judges and mission planning, not just raw detection data.
