What the GCS Should Always Show

The display should always present the flight boundary, current UAV position, mission progress, altitude, ground speed, and essential system health data. Task-relevant outputs such as target locations and mapping information should remain visible so decisions are explainable and safe.

Design Goal

The goal of the GCS is not only to display data, but to reduce operator load while preserving awareness. A clear interface improves mission execution, safety, and judge-facing professionalism during competition.
